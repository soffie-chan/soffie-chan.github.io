My Goals
-----------------

I see myself committed to researching the nature and functioning of the human mind. I hope to utilize my knowledge of cognition to develop impactful video games that serve to help, educate, and inform players of important topics that aren't always well-addressed in society. Currently, I am working on a game that reflects the fragility of modern-day beauty standards. I hope to invest my creative and technical skills in a profession that rewards me with the knowledge that my ideas helped someone understand something new about themselves and the world.

Projects

Cherrim Bloom!

April 2024 - May 2024

Team: Sophia Zhang, Soffie Paul

Inspired by the game 'Lights Out', 'Cherrim Bloom!' is a puzzle game where you click squares in a pattern to make the Cherrims bloom. It features a resizable grid and light-pattern change for user customization.

Sophia Zhang and I share our love for Pokémon, which we implemented as a theme for this project. Sophia did the artwork with implemented patterns, and I worked on foundational code and formatting. I'm so grateful to call this my first team project in a computer science course and even more so with such a talented teammate!
