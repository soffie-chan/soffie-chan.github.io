About Me
-----------------

I'm a junior student at the University of Delaware, triple majoring in Cognitive Science, Computer Science, and Game Studies. In Cognitive Science, I specialize in Computer Science, Mathematics, and Logic. I also focus in Game Development! :)

In my free time, you'll find me gaming, drawing, or devouring a mean chicken shawarma.

My Goals
-----------------

I see myself committed to researching the nature and functioning of the human mind. I am highly invested in studying decision-making and learning about how we interact with computers, video games, and technology. You'll often see me interviewing professors and students alike to gain insight and improve user experiences with commonly used software.

In practice, I code educational tools, video games, and websites. I hope to invest my creative and technical skills in a profession that rewards me with the knowledge that my ideas helped someone understand something new about themselves and the world through technology.

Cherrim Bloom!

April 2024 - May 2024

Team: Sophia Zhang, Soffie Paul

Click here to play!

Inspired by the game 'Lights Out', 'Cherrim Bloom!' is a puzzle game where you click squares in a pattern to help the Cherrims, the Blossom Pokémon, bloom. It features a resizable grid and light-pattern change for user customization.

Sophia Zhang and I share our love for Pokémon, which we implemented as a theme for this project. Sophia did the artwork with implemented patterns, and I worked on foundational code and formatting. I'm so grateful to call this my first team project and even more so with such a talented teammate!
